Our Mission

In the midst of the ongoing conflict in Iran, countless families are facing unimaginable hardships. Global Aid for Iran is a worldwide charity dedicated to delivering essential aid — food, medical supplies, shelter, and education support — to those most affected.

We work with trusted local partners to ensure help reaches people quickly, transparently, and effectively. Your generosity can save lives and restore hope.

How Your Donation Helps

  • 🍞 Food & Clean Water Nutritious meals and safe drinking water for displaced families who have lost everything.
  • 🏥 Medical Support Medicines, first-aid kits, and essential equipment for clinics and hospitals overwhelmed by casualties.
  • 🏠 Emergency Shelter Tents, blankets, and repairs to damaged homes before winter arrives.
  • 📚 Education & Protection School materials and safe learning spaces for children whose education has been disrupted.
